About

HUMANITARIAN AID

Reason for developing this platform

It is a chilly winter morning. You wake up and find yourself cosy and warm in a blanket. You got a wardrobe full of warm clothes and sweaters. Now let us imagine another scenario. You live in a slum and on that same fine winter morning, you wake up cold and rough just to know that you don’t even have warm clothes, let alone a blanket. This is an app which will be used by organizations who want to put humanity first. This app will ease the act of kindness and helping others. Cause sometimes we have a lot to give, but we do not know where to give to actually help others. And sometimes we need something very much, but we don’t know where to look for it. This app will do the task of yours by matching your needs and donations simultaneously.

If you are among the privilaged then

this platform will enable users to help others by donating different kinds of things.

If you are among the less privilaged then

This platform will enable users who are in distress or in need can seek help.
